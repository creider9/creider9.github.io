Build Genuine Connections

CAREspondence helps to build genuine connections between sponsors and beneficiaries.

Sponsors care about how their donations are being used by Non-Profit Organizations.

CAREspondence keeps things organized.

Sponsors can view all correspondence throughout their years of providing, and truly be a part of their beneficiaries growth and success.

Be Notified

What is an update if it gets lost in a stack of mail or buried in an inbox?

Sponsors love updates from their beneficiaries, but keeping track of them can be tedious.

CAREspondence provides email notifications to let sponsors know when new beneficiary updates are available.
